## 1.0.0

- Added Artifact of One-Hit World.
- Non-boss enemies now die from any positive damage tick while the artifact is enabled.
- Teleporter/event bosses are protected from one-hit behavior.
- Teleporter bosses gain Overclock stat scaling (health, damage, attack speed, move speed).
- Teleporter fights now scale to 1/2/3/4 bosses by stage and run time.
- Added boss blink behavior (distance + no-hit + line-of-sight break + cooldown).
- Added optional non-boss outgoing damage multiplier config.
